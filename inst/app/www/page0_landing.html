Tutorial

Welcome to the tutorial section! Follow these simple steps to effectively use the Repurpose app and maximize your data analysis experience:

  1. Step 1: Click the "Get Started" button to begin.
  2. Step 2: On the welcome page, click "Continue" to proceed.
  3. Step 3: Navigate to the upload page and use the "Upload Data" buttons to upload your metabolomics and participant datasets. You can download the demo data here to get started.
  4. Step 4: Select the outcome of interest or the primary outcome for which the study was designed.
  5. Step 5: Choose the analysis parameters to customize your data processing according to your needs.
  6. Step 6: Review the generated visualizations to gain insights from your analysis results.
  7. Step 7: Download your analysis results for further use or reporting.

If you need any additional help or have questions, don’t hesitate to reach out for assistance.

About App

Repurpose is an innovative app designed to empower researchers by reusing and repurposing existing data for case-control studies. With our comprehensive tool, users can conduct univariate and multivariate analyses, obtain inverse probability weights, and generate informative plots such as AUROC and volcano plots.

The app's intuitive interface simplifies the process of uploading participant and biological data, including metabolomics, proteomics, lipidomics, and other quantitative omics datasets. Users can easily select covariates and conduct analyses seamlessly. This tool is particularly valuable for omics researchers focusing on biomarker discovery and secondary data analysis, enabling cost-effective and efficient reuse of existing datasets.

Whether you're preparing for your next research breakthrough or enhancing your current dataset analyses, Repurpose streamlines the process to deliver reliable, insightful results.
